Volunteer Experience

I have experience volunteering for a non-profit agency called Fristers. Fristers is a teen-parent program, for young mothers ages 13-25. I first came to Fristers when I was 16 and pregnant. I helped as a Big Sister and now that I have aged out of the program, I volunteer my time every Tuesday and any other day that I can. I have been with Fristers for over 8 years, through the years I have gained experience working with struggling youth and ensuring that they have the tools they need to become succesful mothers and adult citizens.

Additional Experience: I was a Sheriff Explorer for 3 years at the Fontana Sheriff Station in San Bernardino California. In my time as an explorer, I preformed many tasks. I assisted Deputies with paperwork on all my ridealongs, as well as helped talk to civilians and find out what the problem was. I have spent numerous hours volunteering at the Sheriffs Rodeo every year. During my time I also attended the Sheriff Explorer Academy, where I experienced various aspects in the daily life of an Officer. I was awarded wiht a Helicopeter ride in "40 King" the Sheriff's helicopter for my excellent performance as well as a Tactical Officer Award.
